Who is Iggy?

Iggy is the official AI chatbot mascot of ICCT Colleges. Designed as a smart robotic bird wearing a graduation cap, Iggy represents the fusion of technology and education — always ready to guide students, parents, and staff with accurate school information.

Iggy was developed as part of the capstone project "Web-Based NLP-Driven Chatbot System for Automated School Inquiry and Support Services at ICCT Colleges" by BS Information Technology students from the College of Computer Science.

Whether you need help with enrollment, course information, class schedules, tuition fees, or school policies — Iggy is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, so you never have to wait in line or call the office again.
